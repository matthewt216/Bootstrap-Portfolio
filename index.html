About me

Matthew Tran was born in Oakland, California, and moved to San Antonio, Texas at a young age. He later moved to Los Angeles for undergraduate studies at UCLA, majoring in astrophysics and mathematics. Always interested in the subjects of astronomy and mathematics, Matthew Tran aims to delve deeper into the research realms and use his passion to further his fields of interest. Tran has experience in Python, Java, C+, and HTML/CSS.
